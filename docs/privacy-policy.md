# MD阅读器 Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-06

MD阅读器 is a local document reader and editor for Markdown, HTML, and plain text files.

## Data Collection

MD阅读器 does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data.

Documents opened or created in the app stay on your device unless you explicitly share or export them using iOS system features.

## Local Storage

The app stores settings, recent document references, and the last active time locally on your device using iOS app storage. This data is used only to provide app functionality.

## Network Access

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, accounts, cloud sync, or a backend service. External links in document previews require confirmation and then open in the system browser.

## Contact

For support, use the support link listed on the App Store product page.
